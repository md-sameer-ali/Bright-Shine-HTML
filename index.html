About Bright Shine Cleaning Co.

From there, I decided to seize the opportunity to create a company that provides the type of “best-of-the-best” service I was looking for. Our Values

Trust

Integrity is the keystone of our business. Our employees are background-checked, and we are bonded and fully insured. We strive to deliver on our promises and make every effort to gain your complete satisfaction and trust.

Professionalism

We aim to wow you each time we clean for you. Our employees are thoroughly trained and amazing at what they do. We always treat our clients and their property with respect.

Innovation

We are a forward-thinking, tech savvy company that never cuts corners. We use tried and true family safe cleaning products and equipment, and are always looking for ways to increase efficiency and improve our service.

Employees

Our employees are our most valuable asset. We only hire and retain the “best-of-the-best”. We are invested in being a local industry employer of choice by providing excellent training, work-life balance, and competitive pay and benefits.

Community

We are a locally-owned small business. Our goal has always been to stay local and retain the personal touches that only a small business can provide. We shop local and buy products made in the USA when possible.
