About Bright Shine Cleaning Co.

Samantha Sanchez

Founder of Wake Forest, North Carolina-based

Bright Shine Cleaning Co. I started this company as the result of my own search to find a local cleaner that shared my values and expectations. From there, I decided to seize the opportunity to create a company that provides the type of “best-of-the-best” service I was looking for. I hope you find Bright Shine Cleaning Co. to be everything you were looking for in a cleaning service. I’d love the opportunity to make your day shine a bit brighter.
